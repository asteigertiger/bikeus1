BIKE TRAIL REGULATIONS


+ Always wear a helmet and use safety gear.
+ Ride to the right.
+ Ride single-file when others are present.
+ Always yield to slower path users.
+ When stopping for a rest or emergency, move completely off the trail.
+ Avoid wearing or using listening devices so you are not distracted and can be aware of your surroundings.
+ Before passing others, watch your speed and courteously announce your intentions by saying "on your left" or ringing your bell.
+ Don't "spook" children or animals; always yield to them. If passing a horse, use your voice so the horse knows you are a human.
+ Obey all traffic signs at road crossings.
+ Use traffic hand signals when appropriate.
+ Use proper lights if riding before daylight or after dusk.
